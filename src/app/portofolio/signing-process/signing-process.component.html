Digital Document Signing Process

Characteristics

Project Type: Enterprise

Client: Maandag, for client from Germany

Period: May 2024 - November 2024

Overview

       This project was developed for one of Germany’s leading clients, renowned for delivering innovative digital solutions to the banking and insurance sectors. The objective was to design and implement a robust and flexible document signing solution that empowered brokers to manage their documents directly within the system and independently initiate signature processes, without being tied to contract workflows. A key feature of the solution was the ability to send email requests to customers, streamlining the document signing experience and ensuring faster turnaround times. To support the system’s pay-per-use model, a reusable License Service was built, providing comprehensive tracking and management of usage. This service also offered detailed and accurate reporting on license consumption, ensuring transparency and efficient resource management.

       The project comprised 4 microservices, a micro-frontend, and robust event messaging capabilities utilizing AWS SNS and SQS for seamless communication across services.

Achievements

  • End-to-End Digital Solution for Brokers: Delivered a comprehensive digital solution that allowed brokers to manage all their work, including document signing, within a single system. This eliminated the need for external tools, reducing costs and improving operational efficiency.
  • Cost Reduction for Brokers by 30%: The new solution provided a more affordable alternative to existing document signing solutions, offering brokers a cost-effective way to manage their workflows without sacrificing quality or performance.
  • Improved Customer Communication: Integrated email notifications to keep customers informed throughout the signing process, providing brokers with a streamlined and efficient way to notify customers and enhance the overall user experience.

Key Features

  • Centralized Document Management: Brokers could manage all their documents directly within the system, streamlining workflows and reducing the need for external solutions.
  • Email Notifications: Integrated email requests to customers, providing a seamless experience and keeping them informed throughout the signing process.
  • Real-Time Signing Tracking: The signing process was tracked in real-time, allowing brokers to monitor progress and ensure timely completion.
  • Pay-Per-Use Licensing Model: A reusable License Service was implemented to track and manage usage, ensuring efficient consumption reporting and supporting the pay-per-use model.
  • Cost-Effective Solution: The solution offered brokers a more affordable option compared to other document signing tools, helping them save on operational costs.
